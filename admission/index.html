Admissions 2026

Your journey to becoming a world-class tech professional starts here.

Apply Now

Eligibility Criteria

  • Class 12 passed/appearing with PCM (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics)
  • Minimum 60% aggregate (50% for reserved categories)
  • Valid IIAT SAT or equivalent entrance test score
  • Indian nationals / NRI / International students welcome

6-Step Admission Process

1

Register on the Portal

Create your account on the IIAT admissions portal to get started.

2

Complete Application Form

Fill in your academic details, personal information, and upload documents.

3

Appear for IIAT SAT Entrance Test

Take the IIAT IIAT SAT online or at a designated center.

4

Attend Personal Interview

Participate in a one-on-one interview to assess your aptitude and motivation.

5

Receive Offer Letter

Successful candidates receive an official offer letter via email and portal.

6

Confirm Admission & Pay Fees

Secure your seat by confirming admission and completing the fee payment.
