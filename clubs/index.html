Democratic Leadership

Club President Elections

Every club at uGSOT is led by a student-elected president. Annual elections ensure that leadership is earned through vision, dedication, and peer trust — not appointment.

Candidates campaign, present their manifesto, and are elected through a transparent democratic process. This experience cultivates real-world leadership, accountability, and governance skills that go far beyond the classroom.
