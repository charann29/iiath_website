Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the Educator Development Program

EDP is open to all faculty members from partner universities and institutions associated with upGrad School of Technology. Both junior and senior educators are welcome to apply.

The EDP is typically structured as a 6–8 week program with flexible scheduling to accommodate active teaching loads. Each module includes self-paced content alongside live workshops.

EDP is fully sponsored by upGrad School of Technology through STTAR for faculty at partner institutions. There is no registration fee for eligible educators.

Participants receive a STTAR-certified Educator Credential upon successful completion, which is recognised across the upGrad partner university network and adds to your professional development portfolio.
