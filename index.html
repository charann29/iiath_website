Frequently Asked Questions

Curiosity Leads to Success! Got questions? That's a great sign. Some common queries:

What programs does upGrad School of Technology offer?
upGrad School of Technology (uGSOT) offers B.E/B.Tech programs with a focus on technology and applied learning. Our curriculum is designed to prepare students for careers in software engineering, AI, and related fields through a rigorous, industry-aligned academic environment.
What is the admission process at upGrad School of Technology?
The admission process typically involves application submission, entrance assessment, and a personal interview for shortlisted candidates. Eligibility, required documents, and important dates are published on our website. You can track your application progress through the student dashboard.
What are the eligibility criteria for B.Tech at uGSOT?
Eligibility criteria include completion of 12th grade with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry from a recognized board. Specific year-of-passing and board requirements are updated each admission cycle. Check the current application guidelines on our website for the latest criteria.
Does uGSOT offer scholarships?
Yes. upGrad School of Technology offers merit-based scholarships and financial aid options. Eligibility is determined by performance in the entrance process and overall profile. Details on scholarship types, amounts, and application process are available during the admission cycle.
What placement and career support does uGSOT provide?
We provide dedicated placement support, industry partnerships, and career guidance. Students get access to placement preparation, company recruitment drives, and internship opportunities. Placement outcomes and support services are detailed on our website and in our program brochures.
