Frequently Asked Questions

Curiosity Leads to Success! Got questions? That's a great sign. Some common queries:

What programs does IIAT offer?
IIAT (Indian Institute of Advanced Technologies) offers B.Tech CSE programs with specializations in AI & Data Science, FinTech & AI, Core CSE, AI & ML, Cyber Security, and Biomedical Engineering. Our curriculum is designed to prepare students for careers in software engineering, AI, and related fields through a rigorous, industry-aligned academic environment.
What is the admission process at IIAT?
The admission process involves registering for the IIAT SAT (IIAT Scholarship Aptitude Test), which is free to take and does not require JEE. Based on your performance, you may be eligible for merit-based scholarships. Eligibility requires 10+2 / Intermediate with PCB or PCM with minimum passing marks.
What are the eligibility criteria for B.Tech at IIAT?
Eligibility criteria include completion of 10+2 / Intermediate with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM) or Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB) with minimum passing marks from a recognized board. No JEE is required. Selection is through the IIAT Scholarship Test (IIAT SAT), which is free to register for.
Does IIAT offer scholarships?
Yes. IIAT offers merit-based scholarships of up to 100% based on performance in the IIAT SAT. Additional need-based and Girls in Tech scholarships are also available. Course fees range from ₹1,50,000 – ₹2,00,000 per year depending on merit.
What placement and career support does IIAT provide?
We provide dedicated placement support, industry partnerships, and career guidance. Students get access to placement preparation, company recruitment drives, and internship opportunities. Placement outcomes and support services are detailed on our website and in our program brochures.
