8 Weeks  |  Grades 9-12  |  AI Workshop

IGNITE
Internship Challenge

An 8-week hands-on internship program for Grades 9-12 students. Dive into real-world projects, master AI fundamentals, and launch your tech career early.

Register Now

Program Overview

Everything you need to kickstart your journey in technology and artificial intelligence.

Real-World Projects

Work on industry-grade problems sourced from real companies. Build portfolio-worthy solutions that demonstrate practical skills to future employers and universities.

AI Workshop Series

An intensive 8-day AI workshop covering Machine Learning, NLP, Computer Vision, and Deep Learning. Gain hands-on experience building intelligent applications from scratch.

Expert Mentorship

Learn directly from industry veterans and seasoned professionals. Get personalised guidance, code reviews, and career advice from mentors who work at top tech companies.

8-Day AI Workshop

A structured journey from AI fundamentals to building your own intelligent applications.

Day 1

Introduction to AI & Machine Learning

Understand the foundations of artificial intelligence, explore the history of ML, and set up your development environment for the journey ahead.

Day 2

Data Science Fundamentals

Dive into data collection, cleaning, and visualization. Learn to work with pandas, NumPy, and matplotlib to extract insights from datasets.

Day 3

Natural Language Processing

Explore how machines understand human language. Build sentiment analysis tools and learn about tokenization, embeddings, and transformers.

Day 4

Computer Vision Basics

Teach machines to see. Work with image classification, object detection, and understand convolutional neural networks in action.

Day 5

Deep Learning Architectures

Go deeper into neural network architectures including RNNs, GANs, and attention mechanisms that power today's most advanced AI systems.

Day 6

AI Ethics & Responsible AI

Discuss bias in AI, data privacy, fairness, and the societal impact of AI. Learn to build responsible systems that benefit everyone.

Day 7

Building AI Applications

Put it all together. Build end-to-end AI-powered applications using APIs, deploy models, and create interactive demos of your work.

Day 8

Project Showcase & Certification

Present your capstone project to a panel of industry experts. Receive feedback, certificates, and celebrate your transformation into an AI practitioner.

Rewards & Recognition

Your hard work doesn't go unnoticed. Every participant earns recognition, and top performers unlock exclusive opportunities.

Certificates of Completion

Every participant receives an official IIAT certificate, validating your internship experience and AI workshop completion.

Recommendation Letters

Outstanding performers receive personalised recommendation letters from programme mentors, strengthening your college and scholarship applications.

TOP PERFORMERS

Internship Offers

Top performers in the challenge receive exclusive internship offers with IIAT's partner companies, giving you a head-start in your professional career.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

The programme is open to students currently in Grades 9 through 12. No prior programming or AI experience is required — we welcome curious minds from all backgrounds. You just need a laptop with internet access and a willingness to learn.

The IGNITE Internship Challenge runs for 8 weeks. Within that period, the AI Workshop is an intensive 8-day module. You can expect to dedicate approximately 10-15 hours per week across project work, mentorship sessions, and self-study.

Registration details and any applicable fees are provided during the application process. Please click the "Register Now" button to visit the application portal for the latest information on pricing and available scholarships.

The programme uses a hybrid model. The AI Workshop sessions and mentorship calls are conducted online via live interactive sessions, while the final Project Showcase may include an in-person component depending on your location.

All participants receive a certificate of completion. Outstanding performers earn recommendation letters and may be offered extended internships with IIAT's industry partners. You'll also join an alumni network of young technologists for ongoing mentorship and opportunities.
