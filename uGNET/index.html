Eligibility Criteria

Make sure you meet the following requirements before applying.

  • Class 12 Pass or Appearing

    Candidates who have passed or are currently appearing for their Class 12 board examinations are eligible.

  • PCM Stream (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics)

    Must have studied Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects in Class 11 and 12.

  • Indian Nationals

    Open to all Indian citizens and NRIs holding a valid Indian passport.

  • Valid ID Proof

    Aadhaar Card, Passport, or any other government-issued photo ID is required at the time of examination.

How to Apply

Four simple steps to secure your spot in the uGNET exam.

1

Register on Portal

2

Fill Application Form

3

Pay Exam Fee

4

Download Admit Card
