Eligibility Criteria

Make sure you meet the following requirements before applying.

  • Class 12 Pass or Appearing

    Candidates who have passed or are currently appearing for their Class 12 board examinations are eligible.

  • PCM Stream (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics)

    Must have studied Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects in Class 11 and 12.

  • Indian Nationals

    Open to all Indian citizens and NRIs holding a valid Indian passport.

  • Valid ID Proof

    Aadhaar Card, Passport, or any other government-issued photo ID is required at the time of examination.

How to Apply

Four simple steps to secure your spot in the IIAT SAT exam.

1

Register on Portal

Create your account on the official IIAT SAT registration portal with your email and phone number.

2

Fill Application Form

Complete your application with personal details, academic history, and upload required documents.

3

Pay Exam Fee

Submit the examination fee securely online via UPI, net banking, or debit/credit card.

4

Download Admit Card

Once confirmed, download your admit card from the portal and prepare for exam day.
