About the Campus

About SMRU

St. Mary's Rehabilitation University (SMRU) is a pioneering institution dedicated to rehabilitation sciences, inclusive healthcare, and allied health education. Located in the heart of Hyderabad, it combines clinical practice with academic excellence.

Through its collaboration with upGrad School of Technology, SMRU now offers industry-aligned B.Tech programs that blend world-class tech education with the university's commitment to holistic, compassion-driven learning.

India's First Rehabilitation-Focused Campus

A unique institution dedicated to rehabilitation sciences and inclusive education.

Clinical-Linked Learning

Academic programs integrated with real-world clinical practice and healthcare exposure.

Immersive Healthcare Education

A campus environment designed for hands-on, immersive healthcare and tech learning.

Compassion-Centred Campus Life

A nurturing environment built on empathy, inclusion, and holistic student development.

Admission Process

Four simple steps to begin your journey at SMRU with upGrad School of Technology.

1

Apply Online

Fill out the online application form with your academic details and personal information.

2

Take uGNET Exam

Appear for the upGrad National Entrance Test (uGNET) to assess your aptitude and readiness.

3

Campus Interview

Attend a personal interview to discuss your aspirations, interests, and academic goals.

4

Enrollment

Complete the enrollment process, submit documents, and secure your seat at SMRU.

Eligibility Criteria

Passed 10+2 (or equivalent) from a recognized board with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects.

Minimum 50% aggregate marks in 10+2 (45% for reserved categories).

Valid uGNET score or equivalent national entrance exam scores (JEE Main, State CET, etc.) accepted.

Students appearing for 12th board exams in the current academic year are also eligible to apply.
