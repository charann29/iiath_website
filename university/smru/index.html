Submit the IIAT SAT Application Form Online

Step 2

Take IIAT SAT (IIAT Scholastic Assessment Test)

IIAT SAT is a 90-minute test designed to evaluate your cognitive abilities and potential as a future tech innovator. Here's the breakdown:

Test Format:

  • • Psychometric Test (30 minutes): 25 questions to gauge personality and emotional traits
  • • Aptitude (30 minutes): 18 questions testing analytical and adaptive skills and adaptive skills
  • • Mathematics (30 minutes): 17 questions focusing on practical problem-solving

Step 3

Appear For Counselling

  • • Once you qualify for IIAT SAT, you'll be invited for a one-on-one counselling session to discuss your goals.
  • • Based on your performance in IIAT SAT and preferences expressed in the counselling session, you will be suggested a university collaborating with IIAT, that best suits your needs.

Step 4

Confirm Your Admission

  • • Apply to your preferred university where IIAT academic program is offered.
  • • The University will process your admission following its established eligibility criteria and admission policies.
  • • The admission is solely at the discretion of the university as per UGC and AICTE norms.

Frequently asked questions

What is IIAT?
IIAT (Indian Institute of Advanced Technologies) is a next-generation engineering institute by InnoFutureTech Private Limited, collaborating with universities to provide industry-ready tech education alongside the university's B.Tech CSE degree program.
What makes the IIAT's academic program special?
IIAT's academic program focuses on practical, project-based learning with industry-aligned curriculum. Students work on real-world projects, gain hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies, and develop the skills that top companies demand, complementing the university's academic curriculum.
Where is the IIAT's academic program offered?
IIAT's academic program is offered at 5+ partner universities across India, including institutions in Hyderabad, Bengaluru, Chennai, Pune, Delhi NCR, Jaipur, and many more cities.
Is the UGC-recognized/AICTE-approved B.Tech degree awarded by IIAT?
No. IIAT collaborates with partner universities. The B.Tech degree is delivered and awarded by the respective collaborating university.
Is the B.Tech Degree delivered and awarded by universities collaborating with IIAT, valid for jobs and higher studies in India and abroad?
Yes. The B.Tech degree awarded by the collaborating university is UGC-recognized and/or AICTE-approved, making it valid for employment and higher education opportunities both in India and abroad.
How do I enroll in the IIAT academic program?
You can enroll by filling out the IIAT SAT application form online, taking the IIAT Scholastic Assessment Test, attending a counselling session, and then confirming your admission at the preferred university.
What is the registration fee?
The registration fee for the IIAT SAT application is ₹1200, which is non-refundable.
What is the IIAT SAT (IIAT Scholastic Assessment Test)?
IIAT SAT is a 90-minute test that evaluates your cognitive abilities. It consists of three sections: Psychometric Test (30 minutes, 25 questions), Aptitude (30 minutes, 18 questions), and Mathematics (30 minutes, 17 questions).
How can I prepare for the IIAT SAT?
The IIAT SAT assesses your natural aptitude and cognitive abilities. Focus on logical reasoning, basic mathematics, and be yourself during the psychometric section. No special preparation is required.
What is the Skill Map in the IIAT program?
The Skill Map is a structured learning pathway that outlines the technologies and skills you'll master throughout the program, ensuring you're industry-ready by graduation.
What kind of projects will I work on?
You'll work on real-world, industry-grade projects that simulate actual workplace challenges. These projects help you build a strong portfolio demonstrating your practical skills to potential employers.
Does IIAT guarantee placements?
IIAT provides extensive placement support including interview preparation, resume building, and connections with 100+ hiring partner companies. However, final selection is based on individual performance and company requirements.
What facilities are available at the campus?
Each collaborating university provides its own campus facilities including classrooms, labs, libraries, sports facilities, and more. The specific amenities vary by university.
Is hostel accommodation available?
Hostel accommodation availability depends on the specific university. Most collaborating universities offer on-campus or nearby hostel facilities. Contact the university directly for details on accommodation, fees, and availability.

Disclaimer: IIAT collaborates with partner universities. The B.Tech degree is delivered and awarded by the respective collaborating university.
